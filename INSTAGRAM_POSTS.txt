POST 9: TIPS & TRICKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🎯 5 Tips for Better AI Prompts:

1️⃣ Be Specific
Don't: "Make a portrait"
Do: "Ultra-realistic portrait, natural lighting, Sony A7III"

2️⃣ Use Parameters
Add aspect ratios, style weights, quality settings

3️⃣ Reference Styles
Mention artists, movements, or aesthetics

4️⃣ Iterate & Refine
Start simple, add details gradually

5️⃣ Save What Works
Build your own prompt library!
