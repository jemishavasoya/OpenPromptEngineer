## 🆚 FLUX vs Midjourney

| Feature | FLUX.1 [Dev] | Midjourney |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| **Prompt Detail** | Excellent with long, detailed prompts | Great with concise prompts |
| **Realism** | Very high, photorealistic | Artistic, stylized |
| **Text Rendering** | Excellent | Good |
| **Style** | Natural, realistic | Artistic, varied |
| **Best For** | Photography, realism | Art, creative styles |
