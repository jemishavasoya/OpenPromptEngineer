# Candid Urban Portrait

## Description
A natural, candid moment captured with soft daylight, featuring a woman in casual attire against an urban concrete backdrop.

## Prompt

```
A woman with long brunette hair and fair skin is captured in a candid moment. She's wearing a very loose white tank top that has a very low plunging neckline, paired with blue jeans. Her gaze is directed at the viewer. The background is a textured concrete wall, adding an urban feel to the scene. The lighting appears natural and soft, suggesting it might be daylight outside.
```
