# Headline Workbench

## Description
Generate multiple headline variations in different formats.

## Prompt

```
Generate 6 headlines for {{offer}}. Mix formats: outcome-first, question, number, how-to, contrast, and curiosity. Keep each under 12 words.
```

## Variables to Replace
- `{{offer}}` - Your product, service, or content offer

## Use Case
Test different headline approaches to maximize engagement.
