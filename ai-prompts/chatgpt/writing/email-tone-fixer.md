# Email Tone Fixer

## Description
Improve email tone to be brief and kind.

## Prompt

```
Improve this email to be brief and kind. Structure: Greeting, 2-sentence body, single ask, sign-off. Keep under 90 words. Email: {{paste}}
```

## Variables to Replace
- `{{paste}}` - Your email draft

## Use Case
Polish professional emails for clarity and warmth.
