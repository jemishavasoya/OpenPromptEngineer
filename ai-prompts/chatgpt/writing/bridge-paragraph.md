# Bridge Paragraph

## Description
Create smooth transitions from problem to solution.

## Prompt

```
Draft a bridge paragraph that transitions from problem to solution for {{topic}}. Include 1 empathy line and 1 preview of the solution. Keep under 80 words.
```

## Variables to Replace
- `{{topic}}` - Your topic or subject

## Use Case
Improve content flow in sales and marketing materials.

Writing
