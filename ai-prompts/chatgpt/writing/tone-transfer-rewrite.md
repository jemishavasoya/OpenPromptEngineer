# Tone Transfer Rewrite

## Description
Rewrite text in a specific tone while keeping it concise and clear.

## Prompt

```
Rewrite this text into the target tone (choose: concise, warm, bold, academic, playful). Keep sentences under 18 words, remove filler, and keep it under 140 words. Text: {{paste}}
```

## Variables to Replace
- `{{paste}}` - The text you want to rewrite

## Use Case
Adapt content tone for different audiences and contexts.
