# Objection Handling Bank

## Description
Prepare responses for common sales objections.

## Prompt

```
Provide 4 responses for the objection '{{objection}}'. Each response: empathy, short proof, one question. Keep each under 30 words.
```

## Variables to Replace
- `{{objection}}` - The objection to address

## Use Case
Handle objections confidently with prepared responses.

## Category
Marketing & Sales
