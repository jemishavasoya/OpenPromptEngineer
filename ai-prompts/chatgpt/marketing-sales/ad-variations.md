# Ad Variations

## Description
Generate multiple ad copy variations with different hooks.

## Prompt

```
Create 5 ad copy variations for {{offer}} with one hook each: number, fear-of-missing, social proof, bold promise, and curiosity. Keep each under 20 words.
```

## Variables to Replace
- `{{offer}}` - Your offer or product

## Use Case
A/B test different ad approaches to find winners.

## Category
Marketing & Sales
