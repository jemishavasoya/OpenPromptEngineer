# ICP Snapshot

## Description
Define your Ideal Customer Profile comprehensively.

## Prompt

```
Create an ICP snapshot: industry, size, roles, pains, desired outcomes, triggers, and buying objections. Keep under 140 words. Product: {{details}}
```

## Variables to Replace
- `{{details}}` - Product information

## Use Case
Align sales and marketing on target customer profile.

Marketing & Sales
