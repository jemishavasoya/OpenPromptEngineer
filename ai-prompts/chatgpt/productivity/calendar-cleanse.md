# Calendar Cleanse

## Description
Optimize your calendar by identifying meetings to remove, shorten, or make async.

## Prompt

```
Review my weekly calendar and propose deletions, async replacements, and mergers. Summarize as: Remove, Make Async, Shorten, Keep. Give 1 sentence per item. Calendar notes: {{paste}}
```

## Variables to Replace
- `{{paste}}` - Your calendar details or meeting list

## Use Case
Reclaim time by optimizing your meeting schedule.

## Category
Productivity
