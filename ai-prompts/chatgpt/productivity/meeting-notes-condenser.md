# Meeting Notes Condenser

## Description
Distill meeting notes into actionable decisions and next steps.

## Prompt

```
Summarize these meeting notes into decisions, owners, and next deadlines. Keep under 120 words and list open questions. Notes: {{paste}}
```

## Variables to Replace
- `{{paste}}` - Your meeting notes

## Use Case
Extract actionable items from lengthy meeting notes.
