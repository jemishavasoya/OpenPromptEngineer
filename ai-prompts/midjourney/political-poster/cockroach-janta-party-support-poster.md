## 🎨 Prompt

```
Create an ultra-realistic cinematic political support poster using the uploaded face reference image. The subject should appear as the main supporter of a fictional political movement called "COCKROACH JANTA PARTY".

Design style: premium modern Indian political campaign poster mixed with cinematic editorial aesthetics, highly detailed, bold typography, dramatic composition, warm golden-hour lighting, patriotic energy, and social-media-viral quality.

Composition:
- Use the uploaded person's face accurately with realistic skin texture, natural hair, and facial details preserved.
- The person stands confidently in the foreground with a proud hopeful expression, looking slightly upward or sideways like a leader/supporter.
- Outfit should be simple casual Indian youth fashion (light beige full-sleeve shirt or sweatshirt and blue jeans).
- Background should contain a huge circular "Cockroach Janta Party" logo behind the subject like a political emblem centerpiece.
- Add Indian tricolor-inspired orange, white, and green graphic arcs and campaign-style design elements.
- Include silhouettes of cheering crowds holding flags and banners.
- Add multiple waving party flags in foreground and background featuring the same cockroach party logo.
- Add cinematic sunlight glow, dust particles, depth of field, soft haze, and dramatic atmosphere.
- Typography should look bold, clean, modern, and highly professional like a real election campaign.

Text elements:
"I SUPPORT THE COCKROACH JANTA PARTY"
"TOGETHER WE CAN CHANGE"
"CLEAN INTENTIONS"
"STRONG LEADERSHIP"
"BETTER FUTURE"
```
