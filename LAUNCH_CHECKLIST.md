## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **First Hour is Critical**: LinkedIn and Twitter algorithms favor posts with early engagement. Get 3-5 friends to engage in the first 30 minutes.

2. **Be Authentic**: Don't just promote. Share your journey, challenges, and learnings. People connect with stories.

3. **Engage, Don't Broadcast**: Respond to every comment. Ask questions. Build relationships.

4. **Consistency > Perfection**: Post regularly, even if it's not perfect. Momentum matters.

5. **Track What Works**: Note which posts get the most engagement and double down on that content type.

6. **Be Patient**: Building a community takes time. Focus on providing value, and growth will follow.

## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ Posting and disappearing - Stay engaged!
2. ❌ Being too promotional - Focus on value
3. ❌ Ignoring comments - Respond to everyone
4. ❌ Posting at wrong times - Use optimal times
5. ❌ Not using images - Visual content performs better
6. ❌ Giving up too soon - Consistency is key
